Brain disease term finder


The study of neurological diseases has taken over time a great importance, generated by the advances in the possible diagnosis, its prevention and treatment.

At present, neurological diseases cover a wide range of information. However, although there is currently a series of guides and protocols on the subject, it is not clear enough the part of diagnoses and the advances that are in them.

This search engine allows searching neurological diseases, which are related to a deterioration in the central nervous system, expressed in psychomotor disorders, memory and learning, behavioral, perception of reality, progressive according to age to reach in many times a senile state.
